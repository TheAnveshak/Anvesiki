Heterogenous catalysis is used as it is easier for the separation and catalyst recovery processes, the area for reaction to take place can be limited based on the area and sizing of the catalysts.
Homogenous catalysis is used as it is easier as it has uniform concentration throughout the mixture, disadvantage is separation costs and size based selectivity and capital costs for reactors.
